Touch Mountains

Touch Mountains - the company based in Tasch, Switzerland. Helps you organize unforgettable trips to the higher mountain in the world! With them, you can see and touch mountains like Matterhorn and Eiger in Switzerland, visit Machu Pichu in Peru or explore the Himalayas and Karakoram.

The website was created as a part of React.js learning. As a mountain enthusiast, I decided to use my skills to create a beautiful website for those who love to climb, hike and conquer the peaks of the mountains. the web design, page layout, colors, added videos, and images are my idea.

Construction Consultations

The project created in 2021 for a Polish construction company operating on the market for over 25 years. Specializes in the construction of detached houses, modernization of high-standard apartments, renovation, etc.

Web technologies such as React.js, Framer Motion, Styled Components were used to create the project. The main goal of the website was to create a readable, modern, and animated website containing the main information about the construction company.

Music App

A lovely music application, that lets you choose and play one of the songs from the playlist. All sounds are available on the Chillhop Music platform.

The app was created in React.js. It is a part of the Creative React and Redux Course (used: useRef, useState, uuid. Added features: play, pause, update time, select song, skip back/forward, responsive design). The web design and the idea of displaying it as a vinyl record is an original project.
